Research Interests

Stellar Activity Stellar Spin-Down Cataclysmic Variables Black Holes

The orbital evolution of Cataclysmic Variables (CV) is determined by their angular momentum loss. For CVs with periods longer than the period gap of ~3.2 hrs, this angular momentum loss is dominated by magnetic braking while gravitational radiation dominates in the short period regime.
